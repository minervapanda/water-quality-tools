        Ionic balance — data validation
        
      
        
        Checks a water analysis for internal consistency: total cations should balance total anions.
        Ion Balance Error (IBE) = (ΣCations − ΣAnions) ÷ (ΣCations + ΣAnions) × 100%.
        0–5% excellent · 5–10% acceptable · >10% re-analyse.
