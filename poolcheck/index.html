How to test
  1. Collect 10 mL pool water in a clean clear vial.
  2. Add the DPD reagent (tablet/powder for free chlorine); cap and invert until an even pink develops.
  3. Hold the vial against a pure white card/paper.
  4. Fill the outline with the vial and tap the shutter.
